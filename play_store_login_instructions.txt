This P2P app requires 2 devices.

Device A:
1. Tap "Create Room"
2. Username: "Host"
3. Passphrase: "123"
4. Tap "Create Room" & wait

Device B:
1. Tap "Join Room"
2. Username: "Guest"
3. Connect to Username: "Host"
4. Passphrase: "123"
5. Tap "Join Room"

Both devices will connect to the workspace.
